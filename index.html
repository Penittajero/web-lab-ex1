🏡 Home

Wildlife conservation is the protection of animals, plants, and their natural habitats. It helps maintain biodiversity and ensures a healthy environment for future generations.

📖 About Wildlife Conservation

Wildlife includes all living animals and plants found in nature. Conservation helps prevent species from becoming extinct and protects forests, rivers, and ecosystems.

⚠ Threats to Wildlife

  • Deforestation
  • Poaching and Illegal Hunting
  • Climate Change
  • Pollution
  • Loss of Natural Habitat

🌱 Conservation Methods

  1. Plant more trees.
  2. Protect forests and national parks.
  3. Stop illegal hunting.
  4. Reduce pollution.
  5. Spread awareness about wildlife protection.
